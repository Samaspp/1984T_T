1984

George Orwell
Book Title: 1984

Author: George Orwell

Language: English

Genre: Dystopian, political fiction, social science fiction

Publisher: Secker & Warburg

Publication date: 8 June 1949

Pages: 328

Brilliant. Disturbing. Fascinating. A Dystopia where history is manipulated regularly to accomodate the present, where you are to think, see, feel, work, behave as the leaders demand, believe 2+2=5 and white is black as per their wishes.Your own family is taught to betray you at the slightest sign of non-compliance. Defy them, even subconsciously, and you disappear, undergo drawn-out gruesome torture with a promise of death. The inner workings of this society, the sheer brilliance and inhumanity of the people behind the face of the leader- Big Brother-is meticulous as it is disturbing. One wonders how much of this is a reflection of our own society.
